This policy applies to the Monique Cruz Mentora® iOS application operated by MC Mentora®, London, United Kingdom. By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information as described here.

1. Who We Are

Monique Cruz Mentora® is the official mobile application for MC Mentora®, a professional aesthetic and wellness clinic based in London, United Kingdom. References to "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to MC Mentora®.

We are the data controller for personal information collected through this application. If you have questions about how your data is handled, see Section 11 - Contact Us.

2. Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Profile photograph (optional)
  • Date of birth (optional)
  • Phone number (optional)

Appointment and Treatment Data

  • Appointment dates, times, and status
  • Procedures and treatments booked
  • Treatment history and progress notes
  • Package purchases and session usage

Mentoring Data

  • Mentoring programmes viewed and interacted with
  • Enrolment interest in laser or massage mentoring programmes

Notification Preferences

  • Whether you have opted in to appointment reminder notifications
  • Whether you have opted in to promotional push notifications
  • A device push token (FCM token) used solely to deliver notifications you have requested — stored only if you grant notification permission

Usage Data

  • App screens visited and features used
  • Search queries within the app
  • Saved (favourited) procedures
  • A session identifier (UUID) generated per app launch to link related in-session events — this identifier is not linked to your identity
  • Device type, operating system version, and app version

Data You Do Not Provide Directly

We do not collect precise location data, payment card details (payments, if any, are handled externally), or biometric data.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Providing the service — creating and managing your account, booking and scheduling appointments, tracking your treatment history.
  • Mentoring — displaying relevant mentoring programme content and facilitating enrolment enquiries.
  • Personalisation — tailoring the app experience to your treatment preferences and history.
  • Notifications — sending appointment-day reminders (at 7 am on the day of your appointment) and promotional messages, based solely on the preferences you configure in the app. You may change or withdraw these preferences at any time in Profile › Notification Settings.
  • Improvement — understanding how the app is used so we can fix issues and add features (via aggregated, anonymised analytics).
  • Legal obligations — complying with applicable UK laws and regulations.
We do not sell your personal data to any third party. We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

Firebase Authentication  Google

Used to create and manage user accounts securely using email and password. Google processes authentication tokens on our behalf.
Firebase Privacy Information

Cloud Firestore  Google

Used to store your account profile, appointment history, treatment records, mentoring content, and app preferences. Data is stored in Google Cloud infrastructure.
Google Cloud Data Processing

Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)  Google

Used to deliver push notifications to your device when you have granted notification permission and enabled promotional notifications in the app. Your FCM device token is stored in our database solely to route notifications to your device; it is never shared with third parties or used for advertising targeting.
Firebase Privacy Information

Firebase Analytics  Google

Used to collect anonymised, aggregated usage statistics to help us improve the app. Analytics is only active in production builds and does not identify you personally.
Firebase Privacy Information

Supabase  Supabase Inc.

Used as a supplemental data store for notification preferences, FCM tokens, and anonymised in-app analytics events. Analytics events are stored in a flat analytics_events table with a JSONB parameters column; rows contain only a per-session UUID and event metadata — no personally identifiable information. Row Level Security (RLS) policies on this table allow anonymous insert only; read access requires a service-role key held exclusively by our internal CRM.
Supabase Privacy Policy

Google's services are subject to Google's own Privacy Policy. Google operates under the EU-US and UK-US Data Privacy Frameworks. Supabase stores data in AWS infrastructure and is subject to the Supabase Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. If you request deletion of your account:

  • Your profile, account data, and FCM token will be deleted within 30 days.
  • Appointment and treatment records may be retained for up to 7 years where required for legal, tax, or regulatory purposes.
  • Anonymised, aggregated analytics data (including session-scoped event records) is retained indefinitely as it cannot be linked back to you.

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK data protection law (UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restriction — ask us to restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Rights related to automated decision-making — we do not carry out automated decision-making with your data.

You may withdraw consent to push notifications at any time in Profile › Notification Settings within the app, or through your device's iOS notification settings. To exercise any of the rights above, please contact us at the address in Section 11. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), the UK supervisory authority for data protection.

8. Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Measures we have in place include:

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.
  • Firebase Authentication uses industry-standard token-based security.
  • Firestore security rules restrict data access to the authenticated account owner.
  • We do not store passwords — authentication is managed by Firebase.
  • Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) policies limit the analytics_events table to anonymous insert only; all reads require a privileged service-role key that is never embedded in the iOS app.
  • FCM device tokens are stored in our database and are never exposed client-side beyond the device that generated them.

While we take all reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Monique Cruz Mentora® is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.
